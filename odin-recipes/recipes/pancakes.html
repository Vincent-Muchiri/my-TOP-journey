Pancakes Recipe

Description

Pancakes are by far my best breakfast meal. They are easy to make with very little and common ingredients and can be accompanied with multiple drinks, nuts, fruits etc. This is recipe serves at most three people.

Ingredients

  • Two cups all purpose flour
  • Four cups of milk
  • Four table spoons of sugar
  • One table spoon of salt
  • A table spoon of vanilla essence
  • Half a cup of vegetable oil
  • Not more than two eggs

Steps

  1. Add the milk, sugar, salt, egg(s) and vanilla essence in a bowl and whisk till properly mixed.
  2. Add the flour to the mixture in steps as you whisk to ensure a uniform mixture
  3. Using a brush, oil the pan and use medium heat
  4. Pour a ladle of the ready mixture to the pan. Once the top is dried, turn it. You can add little oil to the sides of the pancakes to prevent it from sticking to the pan. Taste the pancake and add any ingredients if need be
  5. Repeat the above step
